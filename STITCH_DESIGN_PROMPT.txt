JOBNEST — COMPLETE STITCH DESIGN PROMPT
==============================================================================

Design a complete, production-ready web application called "Jobnest" — a free
job application tracking platform. Design every screen listed below. The design
should be clean, modern, and professional. Use a light-mode-only theme. The
visual language should feel similar to Linear, Vercel, or Notion — minimal,
confident, and highly usable.

==============================================================================
DESIGN NOTES FOR STITCH
==============================================================================

1. The app is called "Jobnest" — it tracks job applications for individuals.
2. The primary blue #2563EB is THE signature color — use it for all interactive elements, active states, and brand accents.
3. White (#FFFFFF) is the dominant surface. Muted (#F1F5F9) is used for alternating sections and sidebar backgrounds.
4. Cards should feel clean and contained — no drop shadows that are too heavy.
5. The navbar is the primary navigation — there is no left sidebar for the dashboard (unlike typical SaaS apps). All nav is at the top.
6. NESTAi is the AI chat feature — it has a two-panel layout that lives inside the main content area (NOT full viewport), respecting the navbar.
7. Status badges are always pills (fully rounded), brightly colored but soft (light background + dark matching text).
8. The overall vibe: calm, professional, trustworthy. Not gamified. Not playful. Clean productivity tool.
9. Font is Geist Sans by Vercel — very clean, geometric sans-serif. Use system-ui as fallback.
10. Every interactive element must have clear hover, focus, active, and disabled states designed.
